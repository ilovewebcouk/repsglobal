# Coming Soon — BD Member Email Drafts

Two drafts to announce the migration from the legacy Brilliant Directory (BD) site to the new REPS platform launching **Friday 26 June 2026**.

---

## 1. Email to existing REPS professionals (BD members)

**Subject:** Something big is coming to REPS — here's what it means for you

**Preheader:** A new REPS is launching on 26 June 2026. Your profile is safe.

---

Hi {{first_name}},

After months of work behind the scenes, we're getting ready to launch the next chapter of REPS — and as one of our existing registered professionals, we wanted you to hear it first.

**What's changing**

REPS is becoming a full professional platform for the modern fitness industry — not just a register. Alongside identity-checked, qualification-verified profiles, you'll get:

- A modern, client-facing shop-front for your services and packages
- Enquiries, bookings, forms, waivers and payments in one place
- Coaching tools — programmes, nutrition, check-ins, progress photos and a branded client portal
- Reviews, retention and growth actions surfaced as a single ranked to-do list
- REPS AI to help you reply faster, spot at-risk clients and protect your time

Everything is built around how you actually work today.

**What's happening to your existing profile**

Nothing is being deleted. Your current profile on the old site will remain accessible at **legacy.repsuk.org** while we migrate everyone across. We'll be in touch individually when your new profile is ready to claim on the new platform — no action is needed from you right now.

**When**

The new REPS goes live on **Friday 26 June 2026**.

You can preview the announcement and join the early-access list here:
👉 https://repsuk.org/coming-soon

If you have any questions in the meantime, just reply to this email — we read every one.

Thanks for being part of REPS.

— The REPS team
press@repsuk.org

## 2. Email to training providers currently on Brilliant Directory

**Subject:** A new home for fitness education is coming — early note for training providers

**Preheader:** REPS is launching a new platform on 26 June 2026. Here's what training providers should know.

---

Hi {{provider_name}},

A quick heads-up from REPS.

On **Friday 26 June 2026**, we're launching a brand-new professional platform for the fitness industry — replacing the current site you'll know from Brilliant Directory.

**Why this matters for training providers**

Verification is moving to the centre of how the new REPS works. Every professional profile will show qualifications verified against the issuing awarding body — meaning the courses your learners complete will be presented, surfaced and credited on a profile clients can actually trust.

We're also building a dedicated CPD and education layer designed to give recognised training providers proper visibility — not a logo wall, but real placement in front of the professionals who are looking to specialise and grow.

**What we'd like from you**

Nothing yet. We're sharing this early so you're not caught off guard when the announcement goes out publicly. In the coming weeks we'll reach out individually with:

- How your existing presence carries across
- Details of the new training-provider features
- Early opportunities to be featured in the launch

In the meantime, you can keep using **legacy.repsuk.org** as normal, and you can preview the public announcement here:
👉 https://repsuk.org/coming-soon

Any questions, just reply to this email.

Thanks,
