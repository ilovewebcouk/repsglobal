3. **Assertion headlines, not description headlines** — especially H/E/G/I rewritten.
4. **Bullets add information** — each bullet names a capability the body did not.
5. **Connective tissue** — three short bridge lines woven into bodies (after C, after G, after H) so the page reads as one argument, not 9 islands.
6. **Kill "professional" repetition** — appears max 2× across all 9 sections (down from 8+).
7. **No defensive openers** — section H asserts instead of denying "chatbot".
8. **Distinct emotional pitch per section** — trust, money in, money kept, control, leverage, time back, retention, intelligence, growth.
9. **Section I led by Next Move card**, not generic analytics.
